<h1>Task Overview: Planner and Automation</h1>

<p>In the top-right corner of the screen, you’ll see a planner.
It shows when each task (marked with a capital letter) is active, using a timeline.</p>

<p>Use the planner to manage your attention and distribute your effort wisely.</p>


<h1>任务总览：任务计划与自动化系统</h1>

<p>在屏幕右上角，你会看到一个任务计划器。
它通过时间线显示每个任务（用大写字母表示）处于活动状态的时间段。</p>

<p>请利用计划器合理安排注意力和精力分配。</p>
